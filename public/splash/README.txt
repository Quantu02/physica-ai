SPLASH SCREEN INSTRUCTIONS
===========================
iOS splash screens are optional but make the app look polished.
Required sizes (W×H px):
  splash-2048x2732.png  (iPad Pro 12.9")
  splash-1668x2388.png  (iPad Pro 11")
  splash-1536x2048.png  (iPad)
  splash-1125x2436.png  (iPhone X/11/12/13)
  splash-1242x2208.png  (iPhone Plus)
  splash-750x1334.png   (iPhone 6/7/8)
  splash-640x1136.png   (iPhone SE)

EASIEST WAY:
Use https://appsco.pe/developer/splash-screens
Or create simple dark (#04080f) images with your logo centered.

If you skip this, the app still works — iOS just shows a white flash on launch.
